## What is Kairo?

Kairo is an intelligent learning ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between the fast pace of Riwi's intensive 11-month bootcamp and each Coder's individual learning speed. By combining academic performance data from Moodle with soft-skills self-assessments, Kairo generates dynamic, personalized 4-week growth paths — ensuring no Coder is left behind.

The platform serves two distinct user roles:

- **Coders** — bootcamp students who receive personalized study plans, AI tutoring, and daily activity tracking
- **Team Leaders (TL)** — instructors and mentors who monitor cohort health, review AI-generated reports, and provide direct feedback

## Key capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Adaptive learning plans" icon="brain">
    AI generates custom 4-week study plans based on Moodle scores and soft-skills assessments using Groq-powered LLMs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Focus cards" icon="cards-blank">
    Six prioritized learning cards (2 high, 2 medium, 2 low priority) distill the most critical topics for each Coder.

  <Card title="AI Trainer chat" icon="messages">
    An in-platform conversational AI tutor helps Coders work through difficult concepts in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risk detection" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Automatic risk flagging identifies at-risk students before academic failure occurs, alerting Team Leaders proactively.
  </Card>

## Architecture at a glance

Kairo uses a hybrid backend architecture:

| Service | Technology | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| API Gateway | Node.js + Express | Auth, business logic, session management, EJS templates |
| AI Microservice | Python + FastAPI | LLM inference, plan generation, embeddings, RAG search |
| Database | PostgreSQL (Supabase) | Users, plans, progress, risk flags, AI logs |
| Frontend | Vanilla JS + Bootstrap 5 | Coder and TL multi-page application |
